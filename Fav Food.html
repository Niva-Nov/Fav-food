Pasta

Pasta is a type of food which is made frome dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking. Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine. It can be cooked or baked into many different flavours. Such flavours are basically spices and sauces. I would say pasta at home can also taste the best depending on the time it was boiled for. The magic lies at boiling of it and not all the variety types of things it is later cooked with.


Fuchka

As a Bengali obviously we all love our traditional foods but what more do we love than that is our street foods. Fuchka is one the most popular and widely known street foods of Bangladesh. Not only is it famous in Bangladesh it is also popular in India Pakistan, and Nepal. It also has various other names which is used by everyone. It consists of a crispy fried puff ball stuffed with potatoes, chickpeas, onions, cilantro, egg, and spices. It is served with a tangy tamarind water that is added to the puff ball before eating. Brownie

If we are talking about foods we can't miss out dessert. I feel like without dessert a meal at any time of the day would be incomplete. So as a fellow chocolate lover I love brownies. They hold a special place in my heart.Brownies are a baked goods that originated from America. Unlike cakes, they are not leavened with baking powder, so they are denser and heavier. They are also served in cut squares or bars and are often presented without icing, although they may be served with whipped cream or ice cream.
